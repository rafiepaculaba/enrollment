Contacts Import Vcard

Use this page to import a contact's electronic business card in vCard format from your local machine.

To import a vCard file from your local file system into Sugar Suite, click Browse, navigate to the vCard location on your local machine, and click Open. When the file path displays in the field, click Import Vcard.

The system creates a new contact and displays the information from the vCard on the new contact's detail page.
