Module Loader

Use this page to upload custom modules, new language packs, and custom themes so that they are included in the current installation. You can install a module directly from the Sugar Exchange to your Sugar application. You can also upload the module from Sugar Exchange to your local machine and then upload it to your Sugar application at a later date.

To install from Sugar Exchange:

  1. Enter your Sugar Exchange username and password.
  2. Select the Accept Terms and Conditions box to accept the license agreement.
  3. Click the Plus sign next to Modules to view a list of available custom modules.
  4. Select the module that you want to install.
  5. Click Download Selected and click OK to confirm your decision.
  6. To install the downloaded module that displays below, click Install.

To install from your local machine:

  1. In the Module field, enter the path to the module or click Browse to navigate to the location of the module .zip file.
  2. Click Upload.
  3. Click Install to unzip the module file; to unload the module, click Delete Package.
  4. Click Commit to install the module.

To uninstall the module, click Uninstall and then click Commit.
